Our Process

1
CREATIVE DISCUSSION

To start things off, we will connect over email, Skype or phone to discuss the needs of your project. This will require you to give me a description of what your vision is for your song.

2
FILE DELIVERY

You'll need to upload all the required audio files using a file transfer delivery service such as WeTransfer. This will typically consist of audio stems or project files. For a more detailed explanation, please click below.

3
PRODUCTION / MIX

After receiving all the contents, I'll begin my creative process. The delivery time will vary depending on the work being done. We will discuss deadlines from the very beginning to ensure maximum clarity.

4
REVISIONS

I would recommend all clients to give detailed feedback after careful analysis so we can have effective revisions. I do not charge extra for revisions. I work until you are completely satisfied with your product.

5
APPROVAL

Once approved I'll deliver you the necessary files. I'll also deliver any other data to satisfy artist or brand requirements such as individual stems, project files, etc.
