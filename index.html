The Wiseblend productions is a Mumbai based production house focused on innovative concepts which deliver a 360 degree solution. The combined experience of the in-house team covers music production, commercials, music videos, live events, background scores, training modules, product demos and digital media content.

Our company consists of 4 departments: Filmmaking – For live action productions. Post-production – For editing, visual effects, colour correction and graphics. Audio-production – For original music, sound design and recordings. Content development – For planning and managing assets.
