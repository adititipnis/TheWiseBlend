Why Videos?

A good story can inspire hope, ignite imagination and change the world! At The Wise Blend Productions, we create stories which make an impact. Our team of writers, directors and filmmakers think out of the box to deliver new concepts and original ideas.

SCRIPTWRITING AND STORYBOARDING

A great idea can change lives. Our writers will sketch your ideas into words and our artists will provide you a look into the world of the narrative before we start production.

PRODUCTION DESIGN

Our team of producers will be responsible for the overall visual style of the production. They will play an important role in the creation, execution and management of all production requirements.

PHOTOGRAPHY AND CINEMATOGRAPHY

Our highly experienced team of DOPs and photographers will capture the story with the help of state of the art production gear.

POST-PRODUCTION

Our team of animators and editors will create concise and high quality video content for you.

PACKAGING AND DISTRIBUTION

We can create 2D and 3D animations, motion graphics, digitally optimized content and distribute your video throughout the world with the help of SEO for your video.

Our Process

1
CREATIVE DISCUSSION

To start things off, we will connect with the stakeholders and your domain experts to understand your needs. We will conduct interviews and surveys for consumer research and competitor analysis.

2
IDEATION AND CONCEPTUALIZATION

Co creation and brainstorming sessions with you to create a script and start the pre-production process.

3
PRODUCTION

After finalizing the script and the storyboard we will begin our production process and build a library of content with the help of our production team.

4
POST-PRODUCTION

We will edit, add motion graphics, optimize the colours and render the content to deliver a high quality visual asset.

5
REVISIONS AND APPROVAL

We would recommend all clients to give detailed feedback after careful analysis so we can have effective revisions. We do not charge extra for revisions. We work until you are completely satisfied with our product.
